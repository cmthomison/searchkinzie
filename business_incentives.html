TIF Districts
Tax increment financing (TIF) funds may be available to local businesses for costs that can include property acquisition, site preparation, and other development-related costs. TIF is a tool used by municipalities to finance development in targeted areas by borrowing against a future increase in the area's property tax.

Enterprise Zones
Through the Illinois Enterprise Zone Program, businesses located within an Enterprise Zone (EZ) can take advantage of certain tax exemptions for construction projects and real estate transfers.

Planned Manufacturing Districts
Planned Manufacturing Districts, or PMDs, are blanket industrial zoning classifications that allow for a wide variety of industrial uses but no residential and very limited retail use. PMDs are designed to foster the city's industrial base and strengthen existing manufacturing areas that are suitable in size, location, and character.

For more information on how your business can take advantage of these incentives, please contact ICNC at (312) 421-3941.
